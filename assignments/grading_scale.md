# Grading Scale

## Overall Point Breakdown (out of 100)

| Assignment | % | Points |
|---|---|---|
| Weekly Assignments (10 × 3pts) | 30% | 30 |
| Attendance & Participation | 10% | 10 |
| Paper Proposal | 5% | 5 |
| Annotated Bibliography | 5% | 5 |
| Revised Proposal + Outline | 5% | 5 |
| In-class Presentation | 5% | 5 |
| Final Paper | 20% | 20 |
| Final Exam | 20% | 20 |
| **Total** | **100%** | **100** |

## Homework Conversion

Homework assignments are graded out of 2 points but recorded out of 3. Apply the following conversion when entering scores:

| Score Received | Points Entered |
|---|---|
| 0/2 | 0/3 |
| 1/2 | 1.5/3 |
| 2/2 | 3/3 |
